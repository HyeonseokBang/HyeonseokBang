Overview

In everyday life, people live in pursuit of goals that are big and small. But how will those goals remain over time? Most of them remain digital fragments like SNS posts and photos. I wanted to create a tool to keep my daily goals more valuable. What if everything in our daily goals was recorded by remain a whole process? If these goals are stacked, wouldn't this be similar to everyone having their autobiographies?

Period

March - September 2017

My role

Case is a one-person production application. I've been working on whole process of this project include planing, designing and developing. This was my first iOS app project.

The Challenge


Every goal has its beginning and its end, like our lives. But in the digital world, there is only the present. As a result, our digital records for our goals are simply neglected like a travel photo somewhere in the hard drive, like a Google calendar schedule that's been recorded in the past. Is this the best way to keep our daily goals in the digital world?

"Create digital tools to record, achieve, and store everyday goals."

Plan




The Kipling Method


What kind of goals are there in everyday life? Why we record our goals on a digital device? What are the types of recording goals? In order to generalize the goal, which is the basic unit that makes up the service, I conducted an interview with friends and conducted 5W1H, which are also called “The Kipling Method”.

Who : People who want to be slient
When : People who use digital records to achieve their goals
Where : From the beginning to the end of the project
What : Mobile/Web Application (Most of people use mobile device)
Where : Every data that you need to achieve your goals (Typically Posts and Schedules)
Why : To memorize something to achieve goals, Share information with friends or team member




I decided to call the unit of achievement of one goal a case. It means an event in our lives, like a case in court. it also means to hold something like a plastic case.

Design




Designing a real application


Designing a working app is very different from the concept design. There are rules and guidelines. Elements such as object size and color should also be specified. It takes a lot of effort to make beautiful apps while keeping them. I also studied a variety of real-world app designs, like Facebook and Instagram, to create beautiful apps. Seminar videos on the philosophy of designing apps from WWDC also helped.
